Privacy Notice

Derozan SAS domiciled in barranquilla, atlantico. In accordance with the provisions of the federal law on protection of data held by individuals, makes available to our customers, suppliers, employees and the general public, our privacy notice.

We respect your privacy and are committed to ensuring the security and privacy of your data. We strive to be absolutely transparent in the treatment of your personal data.

The personal data you provide to us are strictly used in the performance of our company's own functions and will not be transferred to third parties for any reason.


Who is responsible for the processing of your personal data?


Which Derozan company will be responsible for the processing of your personal data will depend on how you interact with the Derozan Platform and where you are located. Derozan is referred to in this privacy notice as “Derozan” or “we” or “us”.


Minors


We comply with local regulations and do not allow minors to register on our Platform when they are under the legal limit of the country in which they reside. Parental consent will be required for minors to participate in Nike Experiences and events.


What personal data do we collect and when?


In order to provide you with the products or services you request, we will ask you for certain personal information. For example, when you make purchases, contact our customer services, request to receive communications, create an account, participate in our events or contests, or use our Platform. In addition, when you request specific in-store services, we may ask you to log in in order to provide you with services that will later be associated with your account (e.g., size, fit, preferences).


This personxal data includes your:


contact information, including name, e-mail address, telephone number, shipping and billing address; login and account information, including user name, password and unique user ID; personal data including gender, hometown, date of birth and purchase history; payment or credit card information; images, photos and videos; physical characteristics data, including weight, height and body measurements (such as estimated stride and shoe/foot size or clothing size); physical activity data that has been provided by you or generated through our Platform (time, duration, distance, location, calorie count, pace/stride); personal preferences including your wish list, as well as marketing preferences and cookies.


Your personal data is used for:


1. To process your orders, show you your order history and help you manage your adidas account.
2. To keep you up to date with our new products, news and special offers.
3. To enhance your shopping experience by personalizing the content and communication that reaches you so that it is relevant to you.
4. To improve our products, services and pages based on your feedback.
5. To provide you with assistance.


You can learn more about our privacy initiatives in our privacy policy. If you have any questions or requests, our data protection team will assist you at derozan.sneakers@gmail.com.
